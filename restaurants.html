My Top 5 Restaurants

Created by Aisha H.

Here's a list of a few of my favorite restaurants that are a MUST
if you're passing through


B.B. King's Blues Club- Nashville, TN

In this eatery, you will find good food, good people, and GREAT music.
Let the aroma of Bbq and sound of heavy blues take you to a place of comfort.


Sakura- Hickory, NC

Sakura is a local favorite. Serving up traditional asian cusine and
sushi with a twist. A fan favorite is the "Panther roll!"


Another Broken Egg- Atlanta, GA

Who doesn't enjoy a good brunch after a night out on the town?
Another Broken Egg Cafe caters to all of your breakfast, brunch, and
lunch needs. With multiple locations around town you'll never go hungry😋.


Porchlight Latin Kitchen- Smyrna, GA

Porch Light Latin Kitchen offers authentic latin flavors. The menu
changes daily depending on what ingredients are locally available.
Be sure to make a reservation! This place fills up quick!


Cut Rate Drugs and Coffee- Sumter, SC

Nestled in the quaint downtown area of Sumter, SC, lies the Sumter Cutrate Drugs
and Coffee shop. Serving up breakfast and lunch options, it is a favorite gathering spot
for locals and tourists alike. With pancakes larger than the plate, grab your appetite and enjoy.
Don't forget to visit the pharmacy for some old-fashioned treats!
