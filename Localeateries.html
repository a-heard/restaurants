Local Eateries

My hometown of Hickory, NC is mostly known for
manufacturing and textile industries. Not many know about
the hidden food gems in the area unless your a life long resident.


Highland Avenue Restaurant

Highland Avenue is a swanky farm to table dining experience.
This particular restaurant is located in an old hosiery mill. Boasting bold seasonal
flavors, you will not leave disappointed.



Hatch Sandwich Bar

Hatch Sandwich Bar is one of the newer restaurants in the area. Opening it's doors in 2012,
this sandwich bar quickly became a favorite due the quick and delicious food. Serving up
burgers and vegertarian options, there is something for everyone!
